Cryptographic Puzzle : Warm -up example for practice  
Getting the Puzzle  
You can download the warm -up puzzle using wget.  
 
$ wget 
http://www.cs.ucy.ac.cy/~mylonasy/EPL326/quiz/warmup/warmup.tar.gz 
 
Uncompress the puzzle:  
 
$ tar -xzvf warmup.tar.gz 
Solving the puzzle  
The puzzle has the following files:  
 
details_file.txt 
hash.txt 
problem_file.enc private.pem 
encryptedKeyAES.txt 
 
Your mission is to decrypt problem_file.enc , but for this you need to solve other steps first. You 
should start working on the details_file.txt , which will lead you to the next steps. All files are 
useful, but their actual purpose will be revealed to you in a step -by-step fashion. You will also 
need to use your knowledge so far from the course in order to make guesses. Some steps are not straight forward and may need several trials in order to reach a correct conclusion.  
 
For some steps you will need an ID. For the warm -up puzzle, all students will use the same ID  
(this is NOT  your actual university ID). The actual quiz will be personalized and generated based 
on your own ID.  
For the warm -up, use the shared ID:  123456  
 Each time you solve a step, you reveal a secret token. Sometimes the token is visible to you, 
sometimes it’s not. You should understand when you have revealed a new token by their size.  
 Token 1: 5 characters  
Token 2: 8- 10 characters  
Token 3: 10 characters  
Token 4: 104 characters
